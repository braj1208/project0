Kollam, Kerela, India

I and my friends were going to Thiruvananthapuram, Kerela, India. We studied college here and this was a revisit. You might be knowing that Kerela is very beautiful and refereed to as 'God's own country'. We thought let us make an additional stop and it was Kollam which is 70km from Thiruvananthapuram. I found that it is a quite city with beautiful backwaters. We didn't have much time, so decided to do only one activity which a backwater visit in a small boat. It was a good decision. If you plan to visit here you can reach by following means:

  • Nearby Airport: Thiruvananthapuram
  • Nearby Major Railway stations: Kollam has its own well connected railway station. Other nearby stations are Ernakulam and Thiruvananthapuram.

Kollam can also be reached by road from Ernakulam or Thiruvananthapuram. We got down at Kollam railway station while going to Thiruvananthapuram.
