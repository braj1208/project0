Badami, Karnataka, India

A long week-end was coming during Dushera holidays. Even though it was a festival time, I had nothing much to do being away from my family. So, I planned a trip to Badami, Karnataka, India. Badami is not a very popular tourist destination. It has many old caves and temples of archeological importance. I was interested in this. But, what attracted me most was that it is one of the best places among the world for rock climbing. Many from India and abroad visit here for the same. You can reach here by following means:

  • Nearby Airports: Hubbali(Hubali), Goa, Bangalore, Pune, Mumbai
  • Nearby Railway stations: Badami.
  • Nearby Major Cities: Hubbali(Hubali), Dharwad, Goa, Bangalore, Pune, Mumbai

You can also travel by road in taxi or bus from major nearby cities. I reached by bus from Bangalore.

Being a small town it was very calm and pleasing to sit nearby Agasthya Lake at Bhootnath Temple devoted to Lord Shiva. I also did rock climbing with help of a local guide and I was trilled. Below I share a few pics:
