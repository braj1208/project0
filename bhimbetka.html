Bhimbetka, Madhya Pradesh, India

Bhimbetka near Bhopal in Madhya Pradesh, India houses several rock shelters spanning prehistoric paleolithic and mesolithic periods. Witnessing the rock shelters gives an unforgettable experience of human history, the precise reason I visited there. Someone interested in exploring, or just getting awestruck about human history and evolution may want to come here. So, you can reach here by following means:

  • Nearby Airports: Bhopal, Indore
  • Nearby Railway stations: Hoshangabad, Bhopal
  • Nearby Major Cities: Hoshangabad, Bhopal, Indore

From any of the nearby major cities you have reach Bhimbetka by road using a taxi of a public transport. It is around 50km from Bhopal. If you are taking public transport you will have to walk around 3km.
