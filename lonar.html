Lonar, Maharashtra, India

Lonar is a small town in Maharashtra, India. I read that it has a lake created by the impact of a hypervelocity meteor. So, it is basically a crater! Also, there are only four known hypervelocity impact craters on Earth. I was very excited to see it and very thrilled after seeing this wonder. Following are some useful imformation to plan the trip:

  • Nearby Major City: Aurangabad. It is connected by rail, road and air.
  • Nearby Major Railway stations: Jalna and Washim

Lonar can be reached by road from Jalna or Aurangabad. I travelled from Aurangabad to Lonar by road. Road connectivity in very good in Maharashtra and generally many buses ply between major destnations.
